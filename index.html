Senior High Programs

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Track.

Use and Benefits:
STEM is for students who are passionate about science, technology, and mathematics. It helps students prepare for courses in medicine, engineering, and other technical fields. It is important in daily life as it expands problem-solving skills, creativity, and the ability to find solutions to modern challenges.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences Track.

Use and Benefits:
HUMSS is for those who enjoy analyzing society, history, and communication. It helps develop empathy, communication skills, and leadership — all important for daily interactions, helping others, and understanding social issues.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management Track.

Use and Benefits:
ABM is for those who want to become entrepreneurs, accountants, or managers. It teaches students about proper money management, business, and organization — useful for daily life such as budgeting, starting a business, and leading a group.

ICT

Information and Communications Technology Track — for students interested in coding, hardware, and networking.

Use and Benefits:
ICT helps students learn about technology, computer programming, and troubleshooting gadgets. It is essential nowadays because almost every task is related to technology — from online learning, website creation, and fixing computers at home or in the office.
